Activity Goal Reminders
Get notifications about progress toward your daily activity points goal to help you get up and move more. Go to Preferences » Activity to enable the feature and customize your reminder times.

Notifications
Unread Notifications

• Tapping the Home key on your watch minimizes all open notifications, and marks them as “unread”.
• A notification will remain as “unread” until you swipe to dismiss it.
• If you have any unread notifications, you can now re-open them by tapping on the Unread Notifications bar at the top of the Applet Menu.
• The Unread Notifications bar is only visible when there are unread notifications.

Connectivity

• The Toq app will now show a notification on your phone if Bluetooth was turned off, causing the watch to become disconnected.

Alarm applet
Set silent/vibrating alarms on your watch. Create one-time use alarms, or configure a repeat schedule. 10 minute snooze option.

Timer applet
A simple countdown timer lets you know when your “time’s up!”.

Vibration Setting Changes
For greater convenience, you can now turn vibration On/Off from within the Status applet on the watch. Alarm and Timer notifications will always vibrate even if the vibration setting is turned off.
